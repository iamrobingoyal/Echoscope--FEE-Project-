What is EchoScope? ✨

EchoScope is a productivity app designed to help you stay focused while keeping things adorable. With our cute doodle theme, staying productive is no longer boring!

Features 🌸

  • 📋 To-Do List: Track your daily tasks with cute animations.
  • ⏳ Focus Timer: Study with the Pomodoro technique.
  • 📊 Analysis: Keep a log of your screen time.
  • 💾 Progress Saving: Log in to keep your data saved!
